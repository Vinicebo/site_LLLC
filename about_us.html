About us

Your right is taken seriously here

LLLC is a Brazilian company with more than 30 years of experience in the areas of technological Information, Competitive Intelligence and Intellectual Property, acting as Industrial Property Agent before the Brazilian PTO (INPI) and consultant on Industrial Property and Competitive Intelligence, able to offer to your clients more efficient and complete custom products and services.

With a highly qualified and skilled multidisciplinary team, composed of lawyers, high level academy professors and former INPI examiners, we are able to meet the client demands on analysis and technical advice in all of technical and legal aspects of Intellectual Property.

Direct and electronically interconnected with consultants in Brazil and exterior, in the most diverse human knowledge areas, we realize in the country a unique management model for companies that work in Industrial Property. We act in all of technological areas and in Information Research, particularly with regard to the feasibility of placing process and products electronical, chemical and pharmaceutical products, among others, on the national and international market.

Thus, we are able to offer a monitoring service and technological prospecting for active or not active companies in the innovation and development area, whose goal is to extend the market control or, in other words, weaken the market control exercised by their competitors.

- About our history

The company's founder, Dr. Leila da Luz Lima Cabral, served as a patent examiner at INPI where she also created and was responsible as a head of the Online Search and research service with emphasis on patent documentation to support researchers and patent examination with focus on Competitive Intelligence, until 1992. when she retired.

In 1993, in partnership with Dr. Mauro Arruda, former president of INPI (BRPTO), the consultancy was created to provide industrial property services and strategic information in science and technology.

In 1994, LLC Info Connection was founded by partners Leila da Luz Lima Cabral, Osvaldo Gorgonio Cabral and Douglas Vieira Pinto, assuming the same previous objectives and expanding services by acting as industrial property agents.

In 2014 the company name was changed to L.L.L. Cabral, O.G. Cabral and D.V. Pinto - Industrial Property Agents. Since January 2020, the company has changed its name to L.L.L. Cabral and D.V. Pinto - Industrial Property Agents.
