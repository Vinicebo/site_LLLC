What we offer to the market

LLLC is a Brazilian company with 30 years of experience in Intellectual Property and Competitive Intelligence, acting as an Industrial Property Agent before the Brazilian PTO (INPI). It provides tailored products and services through a highly qualified team, including former INPI examiners, to meet client needs in analysis and technical advice.

With a unique management model and connections to consultants in Brazil and abroad, LLLC specializes in Industrial Property and Information Research, particularly in assessing the market feasibility of pharmaceutical products. The company also offers monitoring and technological prospecting services to support innovation and market expansion or reduce competitor dominance.
