What we offer to the market

LLLC is a Brazilian company with over 30 years of experience in intellectual property (IP), competitive intelligence, and technological information. Recognized as an Industrial Property Agent by the Brazilian Patent Office (INPI), it provides tailored consulting services. With a team of lawyers, academics, and former INPI examiners, LLLC offers expert advice on the legal and technical aspects of IP, supported by a global network of consultants.

Specializing in market feasibility studies and technological research, particularly for electronic, chemical, and pharmaceutical products, LLLC also delivers technological monitoring services. These help clients strengthen their market position or counter competitors, making LLLC a trusted partner for businesses navigating complex IP and market challenges.
